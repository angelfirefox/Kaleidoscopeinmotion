About

Welcome to Kaleidoscope in Motion, where every moment becomes a masterpiece. Based in beautiful Sarasota, Florida, I'm passionate about capturing life's most precious moments through the lens of artistic vision and technical excellence.

With over 8 years of experience in photography and videography, I specialize in creating visual stories that resonate with emotion and authenticity. From intimate portraits to spectacular aerial views of Florida's coast, from vibrant food photography to elegant real estate showcases, I bring creativity and professionalism to every project.

My approach combines cutting-edge technology with timeless artistic principles. Whether you're a local business looking to showcase your brand, a family wanting to preserve precious memories, or a real estate professional needing stunning property visuals, I'm here to transform your vision into compelling imagery.

Every click of the shutter is an opportunity to create something extraordinary. Let's work together to capture the kaleidoscope of moments that make your story unique.
